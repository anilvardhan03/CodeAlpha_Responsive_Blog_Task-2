Tales of Thrills, Chills, and Zombie Apocalypse Adventures

Project - Jijivisha

In the mystical lands of India, where legends and myths intertwine with everyday life, a cataclysmic event unfolds. Unknows to its unsuspecting inhabitants, a deadly virus escapes from a secluded laboratory deep in the heart of the country. Within days, the infection spreads like wildfire, turning ordinary people into ravenous, undead creatures. In this harrowing tale of survival, set against the backdrop of India's rich tapestry of culture and folklore, with bravery, and unwavering spirit will be put to the ultimate test as he fights not only to save himself and his loved ones but also to preserve the soul of a nation on the brink of extinction.
